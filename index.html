A bit about me.

Hey there! I'm Vinit Parikh, a recent college grad, a DJ (some nights), a workout-a-holic, and primarily, a software developer looking for my next opportunity. Although I recently graduated from UCLA with a BS in electrical engineering, my junior year internship at Cisco Systems opened up a massive door to the rich world that is computer science. I observed many fellow interns as well as two of my college roommates as they were able to crank out working products with only two things; a brain and a laptop. I was used to building things in dense lab settings; things that took several long iterations to get working. I learned a whole lot of cool things in college, but decided that I was much more fascinated by the idea of building impactful and fully featured products with a much simpler input as well as fewer dependencies. I aim to work on products that have the potential to improve not just human lives, but all lives around the globe. The most prominent life dream I have is to eventually travel the entire world while adding something positive to every country I visit.
